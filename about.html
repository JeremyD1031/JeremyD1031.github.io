About Me

I am a Computer Science major with a concentration in Cyber Security at High Point University. My journey into this field started when I was 12 years old, and began by a passion for game development. I always enjoyed playing video games, specifically Roblox, which introduced me to the game development world through Roblox Studio. I started with 3D modeling on the platform, and then brought my creations to life with my first programming language: Lua. In the following years, I went on to create several successful Roblox games, which made me want to pursue technology professionally.

My passion became more focused in high school when I took my first actual programming class in Java. These interests eventually led me to High Point University. I chose HPU for numerous reasons, but once I learned about how personalized the Computer Science program was, I became very interested. The tools and resources we have access to like the computer lab, servers, and up-to-date cyber security tools and applications, seemed like they would help prepare me for the workforce. HPU has taken me down many unexpected career exploration paths, primarily through internships and hands-on experiences.

I eventually landed an internship at Disciples Capital, where I work as a Software Engineer programming machine learning trading pipelines. It was a field I never expected to find enjoyment in initially, but it has become a new passion of mine. Outside of academics and work, I enjoy continuing to explore new technologies, gaming, and collecting trading card games. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow both personally and professionally.
